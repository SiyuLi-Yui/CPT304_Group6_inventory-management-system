Privacy Policy

UNIQLO Inventory Management System — Local Data Usage

What data is stored?

This application runs entirely in your browser. No data is sent to any server or shared with any third party. When you grant consent, the following information is saved in your browser's localStorage:

Storage KeyPurposeWhen stored
DATATABLE_INVENTORY_DATA Your inventory table rows (item names, quantities, types, prices). When you click Save Data or close the page after consenting.
app.language Your selected display language (English or Chinese). When you switch the language using the EN / 中文 buttons.
app.cookieConsent Your consent choice (accepted or declined). When you click Accept or Decline in the consent banner.

What if I decline?

If you click Decline, your inventory data will not be persisted between sessions. You can still use the application fully within the current browser session, but your data will be lost when you close or refresh the page. Your consent choice itself (app.cookieConsent) is always stored so the banner does not re-appear on every page load.

How to withdraw consent

You can remove all locally stored data at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage for this site:

  • Open your browser's Developer Tools (F12 or Cmd+Option+I on Mac).
  • Go to Application → Local Storage.
  • Select this page's origin and delete the keys listed above.

After clearing, the consent banner will appear again on your next visit.

Third parties & tracking

This application does not use cookies, tracking pixels, analytics scripts, or any external services that process your personal data. Google Fonts CSS is loaded from Google's CDN for display purposes only; no inventory data is shared.

Contact

This is a demonstration project. For questions about data handling, please contact the application maintainer directly.

存储了哪些数据？

本应用程序完全在您的浏览器中运行。数据不会发送到任何服务器，也不会与任何第三方共享。 在您授权同意后，以下信息将保存在浏览器的 localStorage 中：

存储键用途存储时机
DATATABLE_INVENTORY_DATA 您的库存表格数据（商品名称、数量、类型、价格）。 点击"保存数据"或在同意后关闭页面时。
app.language 您选择的显示语言（英文或中文）。 点击 EN / 中文 切换按钮时。
app.cookieConsent 您的同意选择（accepted 或 declined）。 点击横幅中的"同意"或"拒绝"时。

如果我拒绝会怎样？

若您点击拒绝，库存数据将不会在会话之间持久化。 当前浏览器会话中您仍可完整使用本应用，但关闭或刷新页面后数据将丢失。 您的同意选择本身（app.cookieConsent）始终会被记录，以避免横幅在每次加载时重复出现。

如何撤回同意

您可以随时通过清除浏览器的 localStorage 来删除所有本地存储的数据：

  • 打开浏览器开发者工具（F12 或 Mac 上按 Cmd+Option+I）。
  • 进入 应用程序 → Local Storage。
  • 选择本页面的来源，删除上方列出的键。

清除后，下次访问时同意横幅将再次出现。

第三方与数据追踪

本应用不使用 Cookie、追踪像素、分析脚本或任何处理您个人数据的外部服务。 Google Fonts CSS 从 Google CDN 加载，仅用于字体显示，不涉及库存数据共享。

联系方式
